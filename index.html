A revolutionary breathalyzer to diagnose lung cancer at an early stage

Abstract

∎

With lung cancer being one of the most dangerous diseases nationwide, resulting in more casualties than any other cancer, it has become crucial to design a tool to successfully diagnose it at an earlier stage. Current screening and diagnostic methods are invasive and not cost effective. Our breathalyzer analyzes the different compounds present in a breath sample of a patient and determines the ratios of these compounds.
